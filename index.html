About Us

Welcome to outback nursery & Garden Center

Outback Nursery is one of the largest garden centres on Vancouver Island. Our nursery is situated on a 6 acre property and offers 5 overflowing greenhouses (30x170ft); almost 23,000 square feet. Outback Nursery began in 1998-1999 when our parents retired to the island from Aldergrove. They asked if we'd be interested in joining them and then purchased this heavily treed property. We began clearing some trees and settled into country life trying our hand at raising some turkeys and pigs as well as growing some hedging and conifers. We joined the Farmer's Market, then as our inventory expanded we began running a spec truck down island stopping here and there to show our quality product to potential customers.

Little by little the property was cleared and one by one the greenhouses and irrigation zones increased to what you see today. In the beginning we were limited to wholesale and landscape clientele, then somewhere along the way interest in our nursery grew and we opened to the public.

FAQ

What types of plants do you offer?

At Outback Nursery, we offer a diverse range of plants to suit every gardening need. Our selection includes annuals, perennials, shrubs, trees, succulents, herbs, and indoor houseplants. Whether you're looking to add color to your garden, create a lush landscape, or bring greenery indoors, we have something for everyone.

How often should I water my plants?

The watering needs of plants can vary depending on factors such as species, soil type, weather conditions, and the time of year. As a general rule of thumb, it's best to water deeply and less frequently rather than shallowly and often. Before watering, check the soil moisture level by inserting your finger into the soil. If it feels dry to the touch, it's time to water. However, be sure to adjust watering frequency based on individual plant requirements and environmental factors.

Do you offer landscaping services?

Yes, we offer landscaping services to help you create the garden of your dreams. Our team of experienced landscapers can assist with everything from garden design and plant selection to installation and maintenance. Whether you're starting from scratch or looking to refresh your existing landscape, we're here to bring your vision to life. Contact us to schedule a consultation and discuss your landscaping needs.

How to plant?

  • Choose the right location with adequate sunlight for your plant.
  • Prepare the soil by loosening it and removing any weeds or debris.
  • Dig a hole slightly larger than the plant's root ball and gently place the plant into the hole.
  • Backfill the hole with soil, pressing gently to eliminate air pockets, and water thoroughly.
