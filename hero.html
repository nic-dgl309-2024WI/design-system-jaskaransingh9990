Hero

In web design, a "hero" refers to the primary, attention-grabbing section at the top of a webpage. This section is often visually striking, featuring a large background image or video, accompanied by compelling text, and a call-to-action button or message. The hero section is strategically placed to immediately capture the user's attention and communicate the main message or value proposition of the website. It serves as an introduction to the content below and encourages users to explore further. The term "hero" is derived from its role in being the "hero" of the webpage, attracting and engaging visitors from the moment they land on the site.
