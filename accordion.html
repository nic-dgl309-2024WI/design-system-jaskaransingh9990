Accordion

An accordion is a user interface pattern commonly used in web design to manage content within a limited space. It allows users to expand and collapse sections of content, typically organized into a list of items, to focus on the information they are interested in. Accordions are especially useful for presenting large amounts of content in a compact and organized manner, reducing clutter and improving the user experience.
