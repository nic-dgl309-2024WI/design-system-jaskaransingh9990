Cards

In web design, "cards" refer to rectangular or square-shaped containers used to present content in a visually appealing and organized manner. Cards are versatile elements that can contain various types of content, such as images, text, buttons, and links. They are commonly used in interfaces to display individual units of information, such as articles, products, profiles, or any other type of content that benefits from a structured presentation.
