Carousel

Pagination is a technique used in web design to divide a large set of content into smaller, more manageable pages or sections. It is commonly employed in websites or applications that display lists of items, such as search results, product listings, blog posts, or comments, where displaying all items on a single page would be impractical or overwhelming for users.
