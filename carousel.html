Carousel

A "carousel" in web design is a slideshow component that displays a series of content items (such as images, videos, or text) in a rotating or scrolling manner. Carousels are often used on websites to showcase multiple pieces of content within a limited space, allowing users to view each item sequentially or navigate through them manually.
